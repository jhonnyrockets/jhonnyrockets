Ride Descriptions

American Eagle:

The American Eagle is a wooden roller coaster known for its racing dual-track design, making it a thrilling experience.

Batman:

"BATMAN™: The Ride" is a 55 mph inverted roller coaster that immerses riders in a Gotham City adventure.

Goliath:

Goliath is a wooden roller coaster known for its intense thrills and high speeds, featuring a world record breaking drop.

Viper:

Standing 100 feet tall, it features a distinctive coiling track layout inspired by the Coney Island Cyclone. It's a classic wooden roller coaster that opened on April 29, 1995.

W h i z z e r :

The coaster reaches a top speed of 42 mph and traverses 3,100 feet of track, offering riders a smooth and scenic journey through wooded areas and over ponds.

Dark Knight:

The coaster is housed in a seven-story building and features six sharp 180-degree hairpin turns, creating a sensation of speed and sudden changes in direction.

Wrath of Rashaka:

It's described as the steepest and most inverted dive coaster in the world, featuring a 96-degree drop and five inversions. The ride takes riders through 3,239 feet of track at speeds of nearly 67 mph.

Sky Striker:

Take in unique views of Six Flags Great America as you soar along 130-degree swings of the towering pendulum, nearly going upside down.

Raging Bull:

A fan favorite of the park, this coaster takes you up 200 feet and has a thrilling and fun experience reaching up to 73 mph and lasting almost 3 minutes.

THE FLASH: Vertical Velocity

This coaster takes you up and down 170 feet multiple times and reaches speeds of 60mph, an extremely thrilling experience that has you screaming the whole time.

SUPERMAN: Ultimate Flight

Different to most other coasters, SUPERMAN: Ultimate Flight faces you downward as if you were flying like Superman, and has lots of high-speed loops and twists.

X-Flight:

X-Flight is one of the most fun coasters in the park, with many inversions that are extremely thrilling, yet the ride is somewhat comforting.

Maxx Force

Maxx Force, while only being 22 seconds long, makes up for it through its blistering speed, with a starting launch of up to almost 90mph in only 2 seconds!

Joker

Spins and spins and spins! The thrilling ride Joker will have you spinning in your seat the whole time as it takes you around its track.

Demon

A Great America Classic, Demon, was introduced in 1976 and is loved and hated by many because of its nostalgic significance, however bumpy ride. Be careful not to hit your head!
